How to Trade Cards Safely with Pokemon TCG Pocket and pokemon-tcg.exchange

Welcome to pokemon-tcg.exchange, the Exchange Center for Pokemon TCG Pocket players!
This guide explains how to use our website to find safe, fair trades, and complete your Pokemon card collection.

What is Pokemon TCG Pocket Trading?

Pokemon TCG Pocket is a mobile app where you collect digital Pokemon cards and, since 2025, can trade them with friends. The in-game trading system has some restrictions:

  • You can only trade with players on your friends list.
  • Cards must be of the same rarity (e.g., a 2-Diamond card for another 2-Diamond card).
  • Certain cards (like promos, new releases, or above 1-Star rarity) are not tradable.
  • Trading requires resources: Trade Stamina and, for rare cards, Trade Tokens or Shinedust.
  • Both players must agree to the trade.

How Our Website Helps You Trade

Since you can only trade with friends in-game, pokemon-tcg.exchange acts as a matchmaking and management tool:

  • Build your wishlist: Select the cards you want from the game catalog.
  • List your offers: Mark the cards you’re willing to give away.
  • Enter your Friend ID: Your unique in-game identifier, so others can add you as a friend and trade.
  • Get matched: The site finds users whose offers and wishes match yours, showing their Friend IDs.
  • Manage trades: See proposals, track negotiations, and update your wishlist or offers at any time.

This makes it easy to find trustworthy trading partners and organize trades that the in-game system alone does not facilitate.

How to Use pokemon-tcg.exchange

  1. Select the Cards You Want

    Browse the full Pokemon TCG Pocket catalog and add cards to your wishlist. Filter by set, rarity, or Pokemon to find what you’re missing.

  2. Select the Cards You Can Offer

    Mark all your duplicate cards (doublons) or cards you’re willing to trade away. The more offers you list, the more potential matches you’ll get.

  3. Enter Your Friend ID

    Your Friend ID is a unique code in Pokemon TCG Pocket.
    How to find it:

    • Tap your avatar or name in the app.
    • Your Friend ID is at the top of your profile screen.
    • Enter this ID on our website (no hyphens).
  4. Get Matches and Proposals

    Our system compares your wishlist and offers with other users. When a match is found, you’ll see:

    • The cards you can exchange
    • The Friend ID and pseudonym of the other player
    • Options to accept, decline, or negotiate the proposal
  5. Update Your Account

    You can change your pseudonym and icon to match your in-game appearance, and edit your wishlist and offers as your collection evolves.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find my Friend ID in Pokemon TCG Pocket?
Tap your avatar or name at the top of the app, then look for your Friend ID at the top of your profile screen. Enter this number (without hyphens) on our website to get matched with other players.
What are the trading restrictions in Pokemon TCG Pocket?
You can only trade with friends, cards must be of the same rarity, and certain cards (like promos and high-rarity cards) cannot be traded. You also need Trade Stamina and, for rare cards, Trade Tokens or Shinedust.
How does pokemon-tcg.exchange help me trade?
We match your wishlist and offers with other players, show you compatible trades, and provide their Friend IDs so you can add them in-game and complete the trade safely.
What is Shinedust and how do I get it?
Shinedust is the new resource (replacing Trade Tokens) required for trading rare cards. You get Shinedust by opening packs, recycling duplicate cards, or participating in events.
Can I update my wishlist or offers later?
Yes! You can update your wishlist and offers anytime from your account page. This keeps your matches up to date and increases your chances of successful trades.
